Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical repair take?

Most repairs are completed on the same day or within 24 hours, depending on parts availability.

Do you offer any warranty?

Yes, we offer warranty on both parts and labor. Please refer to our Terms & Conditions for full details.

Which devices do you repair?

We specialize in smartphones, laptops, and smartwatches from major brands such as Apple, Samsung, Huawei, Dell, HP, and more.

Can I get a repair quote before proceeding?

Yes, we provide free and detailed quotes before starting any repair.

What should I do before handing over my device?

We recommend backing up your data. Although we take the utmost care, we are not responsible for data loss unless agreed otherwise in writing.

How is my personal data protected?

We adhere to strict privacy practices and handle your personal information securely, in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

What happens if my device cannot be repaired?

If a device is deemed beyond economical repair, you will only be charged for diagnostics unless otherwise discussed.

Are there any risks involved with repairs?

While every precaution is taken, some repairs (especially on liquid-damaged devices) carry inherent risks. Please review our Terms & Conditions for more details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and major credit/debit cards.

How can I contact you?

You can reach us via email, phone, or by filling out the repair request form on our Contact page.
