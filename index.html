5 Essential Tips to Keep Your Smartphone Safe

1. Protect the screen with tempered glass
One of the most common types of damage is screen breakage. A good quality tempered glass screen protector costs little and can save you from expensive repairs.

2. Use shock-resistant cases
A good shockproof case absorbs most of the impact from drops. It’s a small investment that significantly extends your device's life.

3. Charge your battery correctly
Avoid leaving your phone plugged in after reaching 100%. Try to keep the battery between 20% and 80% to maximize its lifespan.

4. Regularly clean ports and jacks
Dust and dirt can cause charging and audio issues. Use a soft-bristled brush or a gentle burst of compressed air to keep them clean.

5. Keep your operating system updated
Keeping your operating system updated improves your device’s security and performance, reducing the risk of malfunctions.
