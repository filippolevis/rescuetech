About RescueTech

RescueTech was founded with a simple mission: to provide fast, professional, and reliable repair services for smartphones, laptops, smartwatches, and more. Our goal is to get your essential devices back to perfect working condition with minimal downtime.

About the Founder

With a deep passion for technology and years of hands-on experience, I have built RescueTech to combine technical excellence with great customer care. I approach each repair with precision, efficiency, and attention to detail.

Certifications and Education

  • CompTIA A+ Certified
  • CompTIA Network+ Certified
  • CompTIA Security+ Certified

With both theoretical background and practical expertise, I am committed to providing high-quality repairs and support tailored to your needs.
